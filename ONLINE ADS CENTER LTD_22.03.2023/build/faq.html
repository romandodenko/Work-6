Technical questions

Will the tracking pixel put additional load on the site?
We use asynchronous pixels, which do not affect page loading time. Each advertiser is provided with an individual manual with recommendations for installing tracking pixels.
What is a dynamic banner?
A banner is a kind of template - a constructor, which is formed in real time from the information provided in the XML catalog. The solution guarantees the individualization of advertising, in which each of your visitors will see individual content corresponding to his interest.
What is an opt-out page and how do I unsubscribe from remarketing ads?
If the user wants to unsubscribe from advertising, he just needs to follow the link through the I icon in the upper right corner of the banner and follow the instructions on the page with the opt-out function.

What is a lead generation banner?
Rich media format banner with an online application form transfers the completed data directly to the advertiser's CRM system. The solution suggests not to lead the user back to the site or landing page, but to fill in the primary data on the current Internet portal where the advertisement is placed.
