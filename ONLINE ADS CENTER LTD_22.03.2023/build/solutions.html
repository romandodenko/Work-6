Solutions

  • Media context

    - appeal to the audience based on search queries. The advertising message is generated individually based on the contextual request.

  • LAL targeting

    — appeal to a segment (look-a-like) of a similar audience of the advertiser's resource, built on the basis of audience data (social demographic, geo, interests, behavior, etc.).

  • Promo retargeting

    - appeal to a specific audience of the advertiser's resource, for example, visitors who have made purchases or visitors to a category of goods.

  • Promo CRM

    — appeal to the advertiser's CRM audience. Input data can be email addresses, phone numbers, user device ids.
